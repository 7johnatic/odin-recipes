Scrambled Eggs

Source: myself

Description

These are my famous scrambled eggs. Maybe compared to a chef's they're not that special but they're special to me and I like them so who cares.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • A tiny fraction of an onion (Like if you peel an onion and cut it in half, then take off one of the layers, I'd use around a fifth of that)
  • A bit less than 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Curry powder
  • Some oil
  • I use vegetable oil by the way

Steps

  1. Get a small-medium sized pan/pot and heat it on medium or high heat (make sure it doesn't get too hot though like have a sense of how much time you're taking, and it should be hot enough to make water turn into mercury balls by the time the onion is diced)
  2. Crack eggs in a bowl
  3. Add the salt, a tap or two of pepper to taste, and a tap of curry powder

    (This is assuming that your spices come in the plastic containers, if not... then... jus eyeball it idk that's what I usually do)

  4. Whisk for like 30 secs - 1 min or until mixed
  5. Dice the bit of onion
  6. Add some oil to the pan till the bottom is coated and you can whirl it around and stuff. The oil should have ripples
  7. Add the diced onion, move them around so they're evenly cooked until edges turn brown
  8. Add eggs, let them band together as a group by mixing every 15 seconds or so use your discretion
  9. Once they start turning solid I turn off the heat and move them around until the edges start turning a very light brown
  10. Then you're all done, can be served with toast or toasted croissant or waffles or pancakes or yams or really anything if you want eggs are fantastic
