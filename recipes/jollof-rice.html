Jollof Rice

Description

One of the most popular West African Dishes. Even if you aren't from Africa you have probably heard of this.

Ingredients

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 large tomato
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 1 fresno chili or jalapeno
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 cups water

Rice

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1 tsp salt, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp chicken bouillon paste
